Mini-Project #1 - All about you!

You must create an About Me page for next class that has the following:
  1. Your name
  2. a photo
  3. your grade
  4. your email address
  5. Five links that you use on a regular basis

You must also include a biography describing your school history. Specifically, I'd like answers to the following questions:
  • What different schools have you attended, and in what places?
  • What teacher had the greatest influence on you? Why?
  • What are your favorite things to learn in school?
  • What are the biggest challenges you have as a student?
  • What do you most want to make with the knowledge you will learn in this class?
Your page should include at least one paragraph, one image, a horizontal line, five links, and two different header sizes.

Rubric:

1You submit an assignment, but it does not meet the requirements above.
4You submit an assignment that has all of the requirements above. It contains at least one paragraph element, five anchor/link elements, an image, two different heading types (h1, h2, ...), and a horizontal line somewhere.
5You submit an assignment that has all of the above requirements. It also has no spelling mistakes and shows at least one use of styling (such as color or font).
